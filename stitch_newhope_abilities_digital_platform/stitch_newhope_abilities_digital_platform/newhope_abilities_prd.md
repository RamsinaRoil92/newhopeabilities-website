# Project: NewHope Abilities Website (NDIS Provider)

## Brand Identity & Tone
*   **Persona:** Trustworthy, compassionate, local (Melbourne), and highly professional.
*   **Tone:** Person-Centred, Empowering, Warm, Respectful, and Active.
*   **Language:** Clear, Plain English. Avoid jargon. Use "you" and "we".
*   **Core Values:** Dignity, Independence, Reliability, Community Integration.

## Target Audience
*   NDIS participants in Melbourne.
*   Families and informal carers.
*   Local Area Coordinators (LACs) and Support Coordinators.

## Visual & Accessibility Requirements
*   **Colors:** Deep blues, soft greens, warm earthy neutrals. Avoid clinical/alarming colors.
*   **Typography:** Modern sans-serif, high legibility (dyslexia-friendly).
*   **Imagery:** Authentic Melbourne community scenes, genuine interactions.
*   **Compliance:** Strict adherence to WCAG (high contrast, scalable text).

## Core Services
1.  **Community Access:** Building confidence and social connections.
2.  **Self-Care:** Dignified assistance with daily routines.
3.  **Cleaning:** Hygienic and comfortable living environments.
4.  **Household Tasks:** Practical help with chores and meal prep.

## Screen Map
1.  **Homepage:** Hero section with clear value prop, service highlights, and "Local Melbourne" focus.
2.  **Services Page:** Detailed cards for each NDIS service category using the provided descriptions.
3.  **About Us:** Narrative on the "Person-Centred" approach and community roots.
4.  **Contact:** Accessible form for enquiries and referrals.
